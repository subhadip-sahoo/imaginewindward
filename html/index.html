different approach to home building...

Builders seldom use words like "feel" or "imagine if we could...", but at Windward that's exactly how we approach building. With an open mind, ready to create something that is uniquely yours, it is our job to help you imagine your home to life!

Let's face it... making the decision to build is a leap of faith. There are a lot of unknowns to be discussed. Our expertise lies in educating our clients. It is our job to make sure that you understand every facet of your homes design and features. Our knowledge represents thousands of homes built right here in SW Florida. We believe that the Southwest Florida lifestyle involves "blurring the lines between indoor and outdoor living" and that is evident in our designs.

We ask you to join us at "the intersection of building and imagination"!
